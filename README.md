# Streams

A living aquarium where one school of fish flows through three styles of art — sumi-e ink wash, bioluminescent deep sea, and cut-paper diorama. The styles dissolve into each other; the fish never notice.

**Watch:** [techrabbi.org/streams](https://techrabbi.org/streams)

Click the water to feed them. Mind the serpent. Everything else, you'll have to discover.
